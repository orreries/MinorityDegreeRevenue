# Public Institution Revenue Data (HBCU and PWIs)

This is a complilation of analyses and visualizations on public institution revenue. These works inspect 20 years worth of public revenue data gathered from IPEDS. A primary topic of focus in this research is to see the dynamics in public funding for minority serving institutions and also how minority degree attainment effects revenues. Overall this is a comprehensive look at how funding for public institutions have changed within the past 20 years and can serve as a basis for further study into public policy, educational inequality, and broader socioeconomic trends that affect funding and operation of public institutions.
